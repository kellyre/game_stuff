Here is a collection of games and other stuff for your enjoyment.

Spanish Matching Game

Flip a card, then hunt for its translation. Eight pairs on a 4×4 grid, drawn from ~60 common Spanish words — or paste in your own list. You’re scored on time, tries, and how many matches you forgot.

Emergency Compliment

In case of low self-esteem, break glass. A wall-mounted case with a hammer and a single, weirdly specific compliment behind the pane — break it, get told something nice, reset, repeat.

Floor 13
