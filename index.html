Games and Stuff

Here is a collection of games and other stuff for your enjoyment.

Spanish Matching Game

Flip a card, then hunt for its translation. Eight pairs on a 4×4 grid, drawn from ~60 common Spanish words — or paste in your own list. You’re scored on time, tries, and how many matches you forgot.

Play →

Emergency Compliment

In case of low self-esteem, break glass. A wall-mounted case with a hammer and a single, weirdly specific compliment behind the pane — break it, get told something nice, reset, repeat.

Break glass →

Floor 13

Ride a deadpan office-building elevator through odd little floor vignettes. The panel skips 13, naturally, unless you ask it the wrong way for long enough.

Call elevator →

The Deliberator

Feed a grave dilemma into a stern console, press the big red button, and wait through twitching gauges and bureaucratic status updates for a completely mundane printed decision.

Overthink it →

Chess Chase

A Pac-Man-style maze chase where the ghosts are chess pieces — each moves by its own rules. Grab the dots, drop bombs in their path, and blast them with a short-range zap while the rook and queen come sliding.

Give chase →

Tide Pool Radio

Drop pebbles into a moonlit tide pool to tune hermit crabs, limpets, anemones, bottle caps, and other tiny stations. Overlapping ripples can cross the frequencies into briny interference.

Tune the barnacles →

Parcel Panic

Sort a late-night flood of color-coded packages before they cross the deadline line. Aim a route stamp, build combos, and dash through the drones.

Start shift →

Lamp Moth Rodeo

Drift a fuzzy moth around porch lamps to steal glow, push your heat luck, dodge spiders and swatters, and dash away before becoming dust.

Steal glow →

Lost & Found Weather

Sort umbrellas, pocket moons, receipt clouds, and other misplaced things into a three-slot counter, then file the tiny surreal forecast they brought in.

File weather →

Switchboard Sprint

Dash across a tiny municipal switchboard, connect polite urgent callers to the right desks, tap hold when panic blinks, and keep the cords from sparking.

Take calls →
